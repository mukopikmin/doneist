About Doneist

Doneist is the app for managing tasks you have completed in Tooist.

Todoist the greatest app for managing tasks.

What is needed to use Doneist ?

You need to have Todoist account. And the account have to be a premium account or business account.
This app use the API limited to use for premium or business account to read completed tasks.

Start using Doneist !

Sign in Doneist via Todoist OAuth API, and start managing your job history !
